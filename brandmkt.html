In today's digital age, mobile applications are an integral part of our lives, and app stores such as Google PlayStore and Apple App Store play a crucial role in the success of an application. To maintain and enhance the brand value of mobile applications, it is essential to ensure that their meta data and market screenshots accurately reflect the strengths and unique features of the app.

This project aims to initiate the renewal of the Google PlayStore and Apple App Store application meta data and market screenshot for a Com2uS's flagship mobile game title Summoners war. The objective is to highlight the strengths of the game and maintain its brand value across the channel.
